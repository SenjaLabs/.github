## ğŸš€ Overview

**Senja** is a permissionless, cross-chain lending and borrowing protocol built with a **community-first** to unlock global liquidity without fragmentation.

We enable users to borrow, lend, and manage positions seamlessly across multiple chains without closing positions, without complex bridges, and without sacrificing capital efficiency.

Senja is built for a future where cross-chain assets are native, composable, and liquid by default.

Our architecture focuses on:

- **Cross-Chain Lending**. Unified liquidity across chains powered by LayerZero secure messaging.
- **Seamless Collateral Mobility**. Trade and rebalance collateral across chains without position resets.
- **Modular DeFi Stack**. Designed for scalability, integrations, and next-generation financial primitives.
